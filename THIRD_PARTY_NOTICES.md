## FFmpeg

Wispclip uses [FFmpeg](https://ffmpeg.org/) for capture, encoding, and export. Wispclip does not bundle or download FFmpeg — you download it yourself from the [official FFmpeg downloads page](https://ffmpeg.org/download.html) and place it in `tools\` (or point Wispclip at it via Settings). The essentials build from [gyan.dev](https://www.gyan.dev/ffmpeg/builds/) is GPL-licensed. See [FFmpeg legal information](https://ffmpeg.org/legal.html).

You are responsible for complying with the license of whichever FFmpeg build you supply.
